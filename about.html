Jim McCall

President of McCall Architects Inc. is a LEED AP certified licensed Hawaii architect, has a degree in mechanical engineering and a 40 year background in hand-on luxury residential building construction. Jim started out as a carpenters apprentice in high school, worked as a renovation contractor during college, was president of southern Vermont's premier custom design-builder home / general contractor employing over 2 dozen tradespeople and designed quality energy efficient residences in New York, New England and California. He moved to Maui in 1984, built an island style home for himself in Maui Meadows and has had the opportunity to design dozens of beautiful custom homes, retail stores, restaurants and commercial projects throughout the islands.

Jim works personally with each client on every aspect of their home from budgeting, site selection and conceptual design to every minute detail from structural design, cabinet design through custom furniture design using photo-realistic 3D software that lets you view the entire project during the entire design process. He helps you select the best contractor to build your home, is on site working as your owners agent with the contractor during the entire construction process through completion and move in.

Hawaii Architecture

The optimal Hawaii residential design is unique for each site, microclimate and the client's needs, preferences and budget.

Contemporary Hawaii architectural design has evolved organically and is influenced by many architectural styles that respond to the climate and history of Hawaii including early Polynesian Island Style, Japanese/ Asian, Plantation Style, Mediterranean and Modern Island Architecture.

Fine Hawaii architecture form and function responds to the tremendous natural beauty, wonderful climate, and flowing indoor-outdoor lifestyle. Hawaii homes benefit from large open spaces with roll-back doorways and windows opening to views and providing excellent airflow, large shading overhangs and covered lanais that seamlessly connect with the interior living space while incorporating high tech automation, security, energy management and climate control including solar power, energy storage, automated shading and screening, air conditioning and air purification.

Construction methods, materials, water proofing and structural design by McCall Architects Inc. Includes our 33 years of Hawaii design and construction experience that respond to Hawaii's intense sunlight, north-eastern trade winds, southern Kona winds and occasional hurricane winds, heavy rains, flash floods, humidity extremes, high salt air, the rare earthquake, insects, mold and vog and other factors that are important for owners, building and property.
